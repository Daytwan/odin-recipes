Lemonade

Description

Summers Here. Learn how to craft your own homeade lemonade!

Ingrediants

  • 4 juiced lemons
  • 1 quart water
  • 1/2 cup white sugar

Steps

  1. Combine lemon juice, water, and sugar in 2 quart pitcher
  2. Stir until sugar is dissolved
  3. Chill in refrigerator
